How It Works

No app. No bank account. No branch visit. Just WhatsApp and the spaza on your corner.

Security That Puts You in Control

No hidden virtual cards. No OTPs that may or may not arrive. No fighting a bank that "finds no fault." You lock your own account.

Remote FREEZE

Send "FREEZE" from any phone to instantly lock your Stellar keys. No device ownership required.

Trustee System

Designate a backup phone number. If your device is stolen, your Trustee can freeze your account from their WhatsApp.

Merchant PIN

6-digit PIN required for all spaza redemptions. Prevents social engineering. Owner can set limits for managers.

No Hidden Rails

No virtual cards. No silent spending. Nothing can be created without your explicit WhatsApp command.
